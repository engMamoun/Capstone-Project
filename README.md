# Capstone-Project
 Capstone project dashboard, which focuses on regional sales performance analysis.​  The purpose of this project was to identify sales trends, compare performance across regions, and provide business insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

 
## Retail Performance Dashboard | Data Analytics Capstone Project

**Business Problem**

Retail organizations often struggle to identify which products, regions, and customer segments drive profitability. Decision-makers need timely, data-driven insights to improve sales performance, optimize inventory, and increase overall business efficiency. This project was designed to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business intelligence that supports strategic decision-making.

**Tools & Technologies**

* Tableau
* SQL
* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Visualization
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

**Project Overview**

Developed an interactive Retail Performance Dashboard that analyzes sales, profit, customer behavior, and regional performance using historical retail transaction data. Cleaned, validated, and transformed the dataset to improve data quality and ensure accurate reporting. Designed dynamic dashboards that enable stakeholders to explore business performance through filters and interactive visualizations.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Collected, cleaned, and prepared retail sales data using Excel, SQL, and Python.
* Removed duplicate records, handled missing values, standardized data formats, and validated data integrity.
* Created calculated fields, including Profit Margin, Year, and Category-Region metrics, to enhance business analysis.
* Queried data using SQL to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and regional performance.
* Built interactive Tableau dashboards with filters, drill-down capabilities, KPIs, and visual analytics.
* Developed visualizations to compare sales, profit, product categories, customer segments, shipping methods, and geographic performance.
* Documented the data preparation process and analytical methodology to ensure reproducibility and transparency.

**Key Insights**

* Identified the highest-performing product categories and regions that generated the greatest revenue and profit.
* Discovered significant differences in profitability across customer segments and geographic markets.
* Highlighted products with strong sales but low profit margins, revealing opportunities for pricing and cost optimization.
* Analyzed regional sales trends to identify underperforming markets requiring strategic attention.
* Revealed seasonal sales patterns that can support forecasting, inventory planning, and resource allocation.
* Demonstrated how interactive dashboards improve visibility into business performance and accelerate executive decision-making.

**Business Impact**

The dashboard provides decision-makers with a centralized, interactive reporting solution that simplifies performance monitoring and enables faster, evidence-based decisions. By identifying profitable products, optimizing regional strategies, and improving operational visibility, the analysis supports revenue growth, cost reduction, and continuous business improvement. The project demonstrates practical experience applying data analytics techniques to solve real-world business challenges and deliver actionable insights through business intelligence tools.
